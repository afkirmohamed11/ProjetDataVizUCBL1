About the Project

From Your Laptop to Massive Data Centers

When you run a program on your laptop, send a message, or store a file in the cloud, the computation does not stop at your device. It travels through networks and ends up in data centers filled with physical servers consuming electricity every second. This project aims to make that invisible journey visible.

Measured Energy Consumption
Visual exploration of real power usage over time, highlighting peaks, workloads, and infrastructure behavior.
Environmental Context
Linking energy consumption to environmental indicators to better understand efficiency and sustainability challenges.
